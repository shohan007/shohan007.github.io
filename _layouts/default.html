Shohanuzzaman Shohan

I am a PhD student focused in Biomedical Manufacturing, part of the Industrial and Systems Engineering at North Carolina State University, where I research on developing functional tissue and data analytics aspects of tissue quality monitoring. My PhD advisor is Dr. Rohan Shirwaiker.

I have an MS in Industrial and Production Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology, focusing on development of structural framework for Green Supply Chain in chemical industries. I have a BS in Industrial and Production Engineering from Khulna University Of Engineering & Technology (KUET).

From 2015 to 2018, I was a Lecturer in the Department of Industrial and Production Engineering at Dhaka University of Engineering and Technology (DUET). I conducted lectures on several manufacturing topics, also instructed students on several manufacturing projects.
